Instructions

Let's have a look at the voting process here.

  • Why E-VoteZ?

    Can't physically go to a Polling Station?

    It's common to be away from your voting contituency, be it for a job, studies or you're sick. There are several reasons that keep you from physically going to the voting booths. E-VoteZ provides a simple, hassle-free and safe solution.

  • Month Before elections

    Online Registration

    A month prior to the day of the elections, you will be able to register yourself online. All you need to know is your voter's id, access to your verified email account and a metamask account. Registrations will close strictly one day before the elections.

  • On Election Day

    Vote Online!

    Just like physical voting booths, the voting window will be open for 24 hours, before which and after which voting will be disabled. Anytime within these 24 hours, you can take a minute to vote by the press of a button from the comfort of your home.

  • Post Elections

    Results

    Once the online votes and the physical votes are converged and counted, you'll have the results.
